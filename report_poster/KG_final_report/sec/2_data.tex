\section{Data}
\label{sec:data}

\subsection{Sources}

\textbf{Lakh MIDI Dataset (LMD)}~\cite{raffel2016lakh} provides MIDI files aligned to audio tracks via Dynamic Time Warping (DTW) on chroma representations. We retain matches with a DTW similarity score $\geq 0.55$, yielding 19,037 unique tracks after deduplication.

\textbf{Million Song Dataset (MSD)}~\cite{msd2011} supplies audio-derived scalars (tempo, key, loudness, time signature), artist metadata, genre tags with weights, and the Echo Nest Taste Profile~\cite{echonest2011}: approximately 48 million user--song interaction triplets (\texttt{user\_id}, \texttt{song\_id}, \texttt{play\_count}).

\subsection{Processing Pipeline}

\paragraph{Interaction filtering.} Raw Taste Profile interactions are cleaned in three stages (Table~\ref{tab:filtering}): (1)~18,913 erroneous song IDs from the MSD mismatch list are removed; (2)~interactions are inner-joined with the LMD-matched track set; (3)~users with fewer than 5 remaining interactions are dropped to address cold-start. The resulting dataset covers 285,037 users interacting with 7,227 unique songs.

\paragraph{Symbolic feature extraction.} jSymbolic~\cite{mckay2018jsymbolic} and music21~\cite{music21docs} are applied to all 19,037 MIDI files; 7,013 succeed and form the basis for all downstream modelling. Together they produce 1,525 numerical descriptors per track spanning seven categories: pitch, melodic, harmonic, rhythmic, dynamics, textural, and instrumentation.
